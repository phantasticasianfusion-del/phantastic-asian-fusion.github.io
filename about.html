Our Phantastic Family Story

Phantastic Asian Fusion started as a simple family idea: share the Korean and Vietnamese flavors we grew up with at local festivals and pop-up events. What began as a small booth has turned into a family project where everyone has a role – from cooking and prepping, to running the tent, to greeting customers with a smile.

Our goal is to bring comforting, street-style Asian food to our community in a fun, welcoming way. Every dish on our menu has a story behind it, whether it’s based on a family recipe, a favorite childhood snack, or a fusion twist we created together.

Meet the Family Behind the Booth

Our Parents, Our Chefs

The heart of Phantastic Asian Fusion is our parents in the kitchen. From simmering broths to marinating bulgogi, they bring the flavors, techniques, and care they learned over the years into every batch of Seoul Street Fire Rice Cakes, Saigon Garden Rolls, and Phantastic Crepes.

The Next Generation

The kids help with everything from prepping ingredients, to taking orders, to managing social media and design. Phantastic Asian Fusion is not just a business for us – it’s a way to learn, grow, and build something together as a family.

Pop-Ups, Festivals & Community

You’ll often find us at local festivals, Asian cultural events, and community markets. We love creating a space where people can try something new, ask questions about the food, and feel like they’re part of our table.

Our Dreams for the Future

As we grow, we hope to expand our menu, attend more events, and eventually move from a pop-up booth to a full food trailer or permanent spot. No matter how big we grow, we want to keep the same family energy and homemade taste that started it all.

What “Phantastic” Means to Us

Phantastic is more than a fun name – it’s how we describe the experience we want you to have when you visit our booth.

For us, it means:

  • Family: Food made and served together.
  • Flavor: Bold, bright, and comforting dishes that stand out.
  • Fusion: Mixing Korean, Vietnamese, and other Asian influences in our own way.
  • Fun: A pop-up vibe that feels like a festival, not just a food line.

Thank you for supporting our family and for being part of our Phantastic journey. We can’t wait to cook for you at the next event.
