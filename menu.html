Festival Menu

Our menu brings together our favorite Korean and Vietnamese street foods, made fresh at every pop-up. Items and prices may change slightly depending on the event, but these are the stars of our Phantastic menu.

Main Course

Seoul Street Fire Rice Cakes $10

Chewy Korean tteokbokki simmered in a spicy, savory gochujang sauce, topped with green onion and sesame. Add cheese by request.

Bulgogi Gimbap $10

Korean-style seaweed rice rolls filled with marinated bulgogi beef, vegetables, and sesame oil. Great for sharing or snacking.

Stir-Fried Noodles $10

Wok-tossed noodles with our house sauce and mixed veggies. Choice of protein: shrimp, chicken, or tofu.

Street Snacks & Rolls

Saigon Garden Rolls $8

Fresh rice paper rolls with herbs, vermicelli, and veggies. Choice of protein: shrimp, pork, or beef. Served with house peanut sauce and nước chấm.

Crispy Fried Chicken Wings $10

Double-fried chicken wings tossed in your choice of sauce: soy garlic, sweet chili, or spicy gochujang glaze.

Desserts

Ube Cheesecake Crepe $8

A Phantastic dessert crepe filled with ube custard, cream cheese swirl, and graham cracker crumbs.

Banana Ice Cream Cups $5

Creamy banana ice cream with toppings that rotate by event.

Beverages

Lemonade Stand Classics $5

Fresh-squeezed lemonade in rotating flavors such as classic, lychee, mango, and passion fruit.

Milk Tea Specials $5

Creamy pandan milk tea, ube milk tea, and other seasonal flavors.

Bottled Water $1

Stay hydrated while you enjoy the festival.
