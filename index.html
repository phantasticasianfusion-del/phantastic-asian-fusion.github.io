Welcome to Our Family Kitchen

Phantastic Asian Fusion is a family-run pop-up bringing our favorite Korean and Vietnamese street foods to festivals, night markets, and community events.

Every dish is inspired by the recipes we grew up with – from Seoul-style rice cakes to Saigon garden rolls – prepared with fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a lot of love.

Visit us at our next event to try our most popular items and discover new favorites along the way.

Seoul Street Fire Rice Cakes

Chewy Korean tteokbokki tossed in a spicy, savory sauce. A must-try for anyone who loves heat and street food flavor.

Saigon Garden Rolls

Fresh rice paper rolls with herbs, vermicelli, and your choice of shrimp, pork, or beef. Light, refreshing, and perfect with our house peanut sauce.

Bulgogi Gimbap

Seaweed rice rolls filled with marinated beef, crisp vegetables, and toasted sesame oil – great for sharing or snacking.

Ube Cheesecake Crepe

A Phantastic dessert crepe with ube filling, cream cheese swirl, and graham cracker crumbs for a sweet, colorful finish.
